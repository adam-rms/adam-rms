# Business Utilities

Business Utilities provide tools for managing your business's day-to-day operations, including statistics widgets, calendars, clients, payments, and locations.

## Statistics & Widgets {#statistics--widgets}
---
Business stats are widgets that appear on a user's dashboard, giving information about the business and a user's tasks on the RMS.
The stats page allows users to add and remove widgets from their dashboard.

:::note Permissions Required
BUSINESS:BUSINESS_STATS:VIEW
:::

![Business Stats](/img/tutorial/businesses/utilities-stats.png "Business Stats Widgets")
*Business Stats Widgets*

To add a widget to your dashboard:
1. Navigate to **Business Settings > Statistics**.
2. Browse the available widgets.
3. Click on a widget to add it to your dashboard.
4. Widgets can be removed by clicking on them again.

Your widget selection is personal -- each user can customise their own dashboard independently.

:::tip
If your role group has a [Custom Dashboard](../cms/custom-dashboards) assigned, your selected widgets will appear below the custom CMS page content.
:::

## Calendar
---
The Business Calendar shows an overview of all projects that are currently not archived or deleted.
It is also displayed on the main Dashboard page.

![Business Calendar](/img/tutorial/businesses/utilities-calendar.png "Business Calendar")
*Business Calendar*

The calendar displays projects colour-coded by their status. You can:
- Switch between **month**, **week**, **day**, and **list** views
- Click on a project to navigate to its detail page
- See project statuses alongside project names (configurable in [Business Settings](./business-settings#calendar-settings))

By default, the week numbers listed in the calendar are the week of the year. However, these can be customised on a week-by-week basis in the [Business Settings](./business-settings#calendar-settings) page using **Week Labels**.

To export the calendar to an external application (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook), see [Calendar Export](../account#calendar-export) on your account page.
